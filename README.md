# Council

This repository provides a standardized process for community members to submit formal requests to the Executive Committee (EC) or Software Security Council (SSC).

It is the official home for:

- **Out-of-cycle funding requests:** Requests for resources outside the normal community funding proposal process
- **Other formal community requests:** Anything requiring EC or SSC review and potential Foundation Governing Board action

## How It Works

1. Submit a request using the appropriate issue template. Fill out all fields as completely as possible.
   * Out-of-cycle funding request.
   * _Additional templates may be added as the process evolves._
3. EC or SSC reviews your request.

## What Makes a Strong Request
- Clear description of what you're asking for and why it matters to the community
- Specific amount or resources needed (for funding requests)
- Relevant context, urgency, or timeline
- Any supporting materials or prior conversations referenced

## Questions?
If you're unsure whether to submit a request or how to fill out a template, reach out to the EC or SSC directly or open a blank issue to start a conversation.
